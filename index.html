Make Brownies
Step by Step Instructions

Simple step by step instructions on how to make brownies, a simple delicious recipe to try as an introduction to baking.

If you enjoy baking look in our gallery page we display a range of other cakes and treats you can bake.

If you are interested you can go to our SIGN UP page and sign up for more step by step instructions for cakes.

Ingredients

  • 454g butter (melted)
  • 900g white caster sugar
  • Large box cocoa (250g)
  • 8 large eggs (free range or organic if available)
  • 300g plain flower
  • Pinch of salt
  • Teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Icing sugar for decoration

Preparation

Equipment

  • Weighing scales
  • Saucepan (to melt butter)
  • Two large mixing bowls
  • Balloon whisk
  • Spatula
  • Sieve
  • Cake stand
  • Skewer (to test baking)

Fully line baking tin (400mm x 300mm x 50mm) with parchement paper, making sure to fold into corners.

Pre-heat oven to 180 degrees (convectional oven) or 160 degrees (fan oven).

Method

Melt butter in a saucepan on a low heat until completly melted. Remove soucepan from heat and allow to cool.

Add sugar, cocoa powder, flour, pinch of salt in a large bowl and mix together with balloon whisk.

Add cooled melted butter, eggs and vanilla extract to seporate bowl and mix with whisk.

Combine

Pour wet ingredients into bowl with dry ingredients and using spatula make sure to get all wet ingredients from bowl. Combine wet and dry ingredients by slowly stiring together with large spoon or spatula until completely mixed.

Fill Tray

Place mixture in pre-lined tray

Using a spatula scrape the brownie mixture into pre-lined baking tray. After all of the mixture is in tray level off with the side of the spatula making sure that your mixture is level.

Place the tray of brownie mixture in middle shelf of pre-heated oven.

Check Oven

Care should be taken at all times when working with hot ovens to avoid burns by wearing kitchen gloves when handling hot surfaces.

Bake for 40 minutes. Check to see if brownies are baked by placing skewer into centre of brownies and if dry and clean of brownie mixture when you pull out skewer then brownies are baked.

If skewer is not clean when checked, place mixture back in oven and repeat check in another 10 minutes and repeat until skewer is clean.

Cut Brownies

Cut brownies into squares

Allow baked brownies to completely cool in a cool ventilated area.

Lift baked brownies from baking tray by lifting edges of baking parchement and place on flat surface for cutting.

Cut baked brownies, with a sharp knife, into squares making sure cuts are uniformly spaced.

Dust finished squares of brownies with icing sugar by gently shaking icing sugar in sieve over brownies.
