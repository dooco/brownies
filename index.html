Let's Make Brownies

Simple step by step method to make awsome treats

Make Brownies - Step by Step Instructions

Ingredients

  • 454g butter
  • 900g white caster sugar
  • Large box cocoa
  • 8 large eggs
  • 300g plain flower
  • pinch of salt
  • teaspoon of vanilla

Preparation

Prepare lining tin

making sure to fully line baking tin (400mm x 300mm x 50mm) with parchement paper, folding into corners.

Melt butter

Melt butter in a saucepan on a low heat until completly melted. Remove soucepan from heat and allow to cool.

Pre-heat Oven

Pre-heat oven to 180 degrees

Assemble Dry ingredients

Assemble the dry ingredients

Assemble the sugar, cocoa, pinch of salt in a large bowl and mix together until the ingredients are completely mixed into a homogenous dry combination.

Assemble Wet ingredients

Combine wet and dry ingredients

Place eggs, cooled melted butter and teaspoon of vanila in a bowl. Gently stir together.


Pour wet ingredients into bowl with dry ingredients and combine by slowly stiring together until completely mixed.

Check Brownies in Oven

Keep an eye on the brownies in theoven

Put timer for 40 minutes and check by placing fork into centre of brownies and if clean when you pull out then brownies are ready.
